# Key Concepts Glossary — Vortex-Based Mathematics

Working glossary of every named concept surfaced from the public sources catalogued in `SOURCE_LIBRARY.md`. Definitions are paraphrased from author-controlled and aligned-third-party material; the organisation should review and ratify canonical wording before publication.

Format: **Term** — short definition. *(source key)*

## Core mathematical objects

- **Vortex-Based Mathematics (VBM)** — Rodin's system claiming the decimal number system is not a human convention but a natural law that, when reduced via digital roots (mod-9), reveals a non-decaying energy/geometry pattern shaped like a torus. *(P1-1, P1-3, P2-17)*
- **Mathematical Fingerprint of God / Symbol of Enlightenment** — A circle with the digits 1–9 placed at equal angles, with the doubling/halving sequence traced as an infinity-like figure. The base diagram of VBM. *(P1-1)*
- **Doubling Circuit** — The infinite repeating sequence 1, 2, 4, 8, 7, 5 obtained by doubling 1 and reducing each result to its digital root (1→2→4→8→16→7→14→5→10→1…). Treated as the "third dimension" of motion. *(P1-1)*
- **Halving Circuit** — The same sequence traversed in reverse: 1, 5, 7, 8, 4, 2 (halving / dividing by 2 with digital-root reduction). *(P1-1)*
- **3-6-9 Oscillation / Yin-Yang as Trinary** — The numbers 3, 6, 9 sit "outside" the doubling circuit and pulse between the two lobes of the figure. Rodin reframes the Yin-Yang as a trinary (3-state), not binary, oscillation. *(P1-1)*
- **Number 9 / Spirit** — 9 is treated as singular: every multiple of 9 reduces to 9; 9 is "the only thing in the universe that moves in a straight line"; identified with Spirit and the unchanging linear emanation from the centre. *(P1-1)*
- **Polar Number Pairs** — Three pairs that always sum to 9: (1, 8), (2, 7), (4, 5). Drive the symmetry of the circle and underlie most VBM relationships. *(P1-1)*
- **Family Number Groups** — Three triads, +3 / +6 / +9, formed by stepping around the circle by three. Used for the Aetheron flow grouping. *(P1-1)*
- **Powers of Ten** — Demonstration that decimal scaling preserves the same digital-root pattern (i.e., 10, 100, 1000 all reduce to 1), arguing the system is scale-invariant. *(P1-1)*

## Geometric / topological objects

- **ABHA Torus** — Rodin's named torus, the dimensional template of the universe. The name *Abha* is taken from the Baháʼí "Most Great Name" (numerologically reduces to 9 via Abjad notation). *(P1-1, P3-24)*
- **Decoqubit** — A "Holographic Hypersphere" / "Divine Algorithm"; the lattice-like deeper structure that the Symbol/Torus represents. Described as the schematic of the universe; the pathway matter and energy take from quantum scale to galactic scale. *(P2-18, P2-19)*
- **Sanctified Mirrors** — Visual/symbolic representations of the Decoqubit; gateways or portals into the universe's architecture. (2023 book title.) *(P2-18)*
- **Rodin Torus / Rodin Number Map** — The 2D number grid that, when wrapped, forms the torus surface and dictates Rodin Coil winding patterns. *(P1-1, P3-22)*
- **Enneagram** — Rodin asserts the classical Gurdjieff-style enneagram is identical to the Symbol of Enlightenment when correctly reoriented. *(P1-1)*
- **Phyllotaxis Prime Number Sieve** — Rodin's mapping of prime number distribution to phyllotaxis (plant spiral) patterns. *(P1-1)*

## Energy / physics constructs

- **Aetheron / Aetheric Polarity** — Rodin's posited fundamental quasi-particle: "linear emanations of quasi-mass/energy travelling in a straight line from the centre of mass outwards". Activates one-sixth of the torus surface at any moment. *(P1-1)*
- **Aetheron Flux Monopole Emanations** — The radiating/inflowing pattern of Aetherons; explains gravity, dark matter, and the toroidal flow in Rodin's framework. *(P1-1)*
- **Rodin Coil** — Copper-wire coil wound on a toroidal core following the Doubling Circuit pattern. Claims include increased antenna efficiency, novel electromagnetic effects, and (proponents argue) propulsion / free-energy applications. *(P1-1, P1-2, P3-22)*
- **Aerodynamic Marko Rodin Solution / Rodin Solution Project** — Rodin's umbrella term for the engineering-application portfolio of VBM (coils, antennas, motors, propulsion, fusion concepts). *(P1-1, P1-2, P3-22)*
- **Spirit Density Mapping** — Topic of recent (post-2022) Rodin masterclasses on his YouTube channel. *Definition needs org confirmation.*
- **Dandelion Puff Principle** — From Rodin's *AERODYNAMICSSS* book; "point energy creation physics". *Definition needs org confirmation.*
