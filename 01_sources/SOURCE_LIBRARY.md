# Source Library — Marko Rodin & Randy Powell

Annotated bibliography of publicly available primary and aligned-third-party sources for the Vortex-Based Mathematics (VBM) course. Compiled 2026-05-07. Authorization: course is being built for Rodin/Powell's organization, so quoting and reproduction with attribution is in scope.

Sources are tiered:
- **P1 — Primary, author-controlled.** Marko Rodin or Randy Powell speaking/writing directly.
- **P2 — Aligned third party.** Magazines, partner sites, interviews where the authors are quoted/featured with their cooperation.
- **P3 — Independent reference.** Wikis, encyclopedic entries, academic write-ups (useful for cross-checking terminology and biography).
- **P4 — Critical / skeptical.** Worth knowing because the course should anticipate and address objections, but not a teaching source.
