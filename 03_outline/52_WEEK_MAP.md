# 52-Week Spiral Map

The full year, week by week. Each row is one **theme**. Each theme runs as **3 modules × 9 lessons × 6 min = 162 min of instruction per week**.

Notation:
- **★ Revisit** — the theme is being seen for the second time, deeper, after a long enough gap that students re-encounter it with new eyes.
- **▲ Synthesis** — week dedicated to weaving prior weeks together.
- *Lens A / B / C* — the three modules each week, mapped to: A = Foundations lens, B = Mathematical lens, C = Practical lens. (Some weeks call out specific framings; otherwise the standard A/B/C structure applies.)

## Spiral revisit summary

Five concepts appear in the year twice (introduction in Q1/Q2, deeper revisit in Q3/Q4):

| Concept | First appearance | Revisit | Why this revisit earns its place |
|---------|------------------|---------|----------------------------------|
| The Doubling Circuit | Week 4 | Week 27 ★ | Now that students have the torus, the circuit *becomes* a coil winding pattern. |
| Polar Number Pairs | Week 6 | Week 28 ★ | Pairs become topological antipodes — a structural fact you can't see on a flat ring. |
| The 3-6-9 Pulse | Week 8 | Week 29 ★ | The pulse becomes an axis — orthogonal, not sequential. |
| The ABHA Torus | Week 17 | Week 40 ★ | After Aetheron, after the coil, after applications — the torus you re-meet is a different object. |
| The Symbol of Enlightenment | Week 2 | Week 41 ★ | The Symbol is no longer "a circle with numbers"; it's a 2D shadow of the Decoqubit. |

This is the deliberate redundancy. Five concepts, two passes each, well-spaced. Combined with the in-week 9-lens structure, retention should be strong.

## What's missing / open questions

- **Approximately 47 named theme slots.** Two are reserved for capstone weeks (50, 51) and four for synthesis (13, 26, 39, 52); one is the welcome (week 1). The remaining 47 cover one canonical concept each. The org should review the list and call out anything important that's missing — particularly anything from the *Sanctified Mirrors* book or the recent Spirit Density Mapping masterclasses where the public web is thin.
- **Q4 weeks 43–48** are the weakest ground. They lean on cross-tradition connections (Tesla, Larson, Haramein) and on Rodin material I have less depth on (Dandelion Puff, PiALOGUE). The org will want to substitute / re-order these.
- **Sequencing within each week** — A then B then C — is the default. Some weeks (e.g., a contemplative theme) might want a different lens order. Flag at production time, not now.
