## 4. Body (1:00–4:30)

> "Two explanations for plant phyllotaxis.
>
> *[pause]*
>
> *Standard explanation (golden ratio).* Plants pack new growth tightly. The angle of divergence between successive leaves or seeds is approximately 137.5° because that's *360° × (1 - 1/φ)* where φ is the golden ratio. Successive growth points distribute as evenly as possible because 137.5° is irrational with respect to 360°, never repeating exactly. This packing maximises sunlight access (for leaves) or seed density (for seed heads). Evolution selected for the angle.
>
> The standard explanation is *biological-evolutionary*. The angle is favoured because it produces denser packing.
>
> *[pause]*
>
> *Marko's explanation (digital root).* The Fibonacci sequence's digital roots cycle with period 24. The Rodin Number Map has 24 columns. The two structures share a deep numerical fact. Plants exhibit Fibonacci because Fibonacci connects to the underlying decimal-system structure that the Symbol describes. The plants aren't *just* solving a packing problem; they're *expressing* the Symbol's mathematical structure.
>
> Marko's explanation is *structural-numerical*. The plants aren't accidentally landing on Fibonacci; they're embodying decimal-system arithmetic.
>
> *[pause]*
>
> Two differences.
>
> *Difference one — what's primary.* Standard: the packing problem is primary; Fibonacci emerges as a solution. Marko: the digital-root structure is primary; Fibonacci is one of its expressions; plants happen to instantiate both.
>
> *Difference two — what's coincidental.* Standard: the period-24 connection is coincidental; period 24 just happens to be 4 × 6 (twice Fibonacci's period of 12 mod 9? — no, the Pisano period mod 9 is 24). Marko: the period-24 connection is *evidence* of structural alignment between plants and the Symbol.
>
> *[pause]*
>
> Honest assessment. Standard biological-evolutionary explanations *do* successfully predict phyllotactic patterns. Models of growth-with-packing reproduce observed spirals quantitatively. Marko's digital-root framing *adds* a mathematical observation (the period-24 coincidence) but doesn't *replace* the standard explanation.
>
> So: the standard explanation suffices for understanding *why* plants spiral. Marko's framing adds an observation about *the structural connection between plant arithmetic and the Symbol*. Whether this addition is meaningful or coincidental is contested.
>
> Useful framing. *Standard biological-evolutionary explanations predict phyllotactic patterns. Marko adds the observation that the Pisano period mod 9 (24) matches the Symbol's column count. Whether this coincidence is structurally meaningful is debated.*"
