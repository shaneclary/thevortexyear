## 4. Body (1:00–4:30)

> "The objection. *We've now seen all nine multiplication series. Some collapse, some make triangles, some make the figure-eight, some make a line. The figure-eight (×2 and ×5) is interesting, but so is the triangle (×4 and ×7). Why is ×2 specifically the centre of VBM, and not ×4?*
>
> Three reasons ×2 is privileged.
>
> *[pause]*
>
> *First — ×2 is a maximal generator.* It produces the longest cycle (length 6, visiting all six units). ×4 produces a length-3 cycle visiting only three units. The longer cycle carries more structure.
>
> *Second — ×2 is the smallest non-trivial multiplier.* Among the maximal generators (×2 and ×5), ×2 is smaller. Doubling is the *minimal scaling factor* that produces non-trivial structure. Smaller multipliers (×1) are identity; larger maximal-generator multipliers (×5) are equivalent in power but typically introduced as halving (the inverse of ×2) rather than as a primary structure.
>
> *Third — ×2 corresponds to a fundamental physical operation.* In electrical engineering, doubling appears as: AC voltage peaks (peak-to-peak voltage doubles peak voltage), full-wave rectification (doubles the frequency), harmonic generation (second harmonic doubles the fundamental). Doubling is the physical correlate of many basic engineering operations. ×4, ×5, ×7 don't have the same engineering ubiquity.
>
> *[pause]*
>
> So the calibrated answer to *why ×2 special?*:
>
> *Doubling is the smallest maximal generator of `(ℤ/9ℤ)*` — it produces the longest possible cycle (length 6, all six units) at the smallest non-trivial multiplier. ×5 is also a maximal generator but is conventionally introduced as the inverse of ×2 (halving). ×4 and ×7 produce shorter cycles. Doubling's privilege is mathematical (maximal generator) and conventional (smallest)*
>
> Forty-six words.
>
> *[pause]*
>
> One nuance. A purist could argue that *×5 should be equally central* — it's also a maximal generator, and the Halving Circuit is structurally dual to the Doubling Circuit. The course teaches both (Wk 4 Doubling, Wk 5 Halving) and treats them as a pair. The privilege of ×2 over ×5 is *historical* — Marko encountered doubling first, and the Doubling Circuit is the canonical entry point. Practical, not principled. Both are equally fundamental."
