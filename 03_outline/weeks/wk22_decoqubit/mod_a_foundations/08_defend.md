> *Level 2 — engineering: speculative.*
>
> Marko has speculated that the Decoqubit could inform engineering. *No Decoqubit-based device has been built and measured.* The engineering claims remain speculative.
>
> *Honest position.* Engineering applications are conjectural until built and measured.
>
> *Level 3 — metaphysical: Marko's framing.*
>
> Marko's Divine Algorithm framing — the Decoqubit as the structure of physical reality and consciousness — is *interpretive*. *Not derivable from VBM math; not testable through standard means.*

> *Honest position.* This is Marko's view; held as such.
