## 4. Body (1:00–4:30)

**[ORG-CLIP]**

> *"Adding three to a number on the Symbol moves it to the next family. One becomes four; four becomes seven; seven becomes one again — that is family one's cycle. Two becomes five; five becomes eight; eight becomes two — family two's cycle. Three becomes six; six becomes nine; nine becomes three — family three's cycle. The plus-three step is the *minimal motion* between families. It is the heartbeat of the family structure. When you trace a number through repeated additions of three, you are walking through one family's three vertices in cyclic order. Plus-six moves you two steps within the family — same destination type as plus-three, but jumping over one. Plus-nine returns you to the same number — the trivial step. So plus-three is the generator of family motion. The plus-three is to families what doubling is to the cycle of units. Both are the canonical generators of their respective structures."*
> — Marko Rodin, paraphrased. **Source:** P1-1, P1-3.
