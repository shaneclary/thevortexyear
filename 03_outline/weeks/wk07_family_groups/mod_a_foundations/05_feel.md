## 4. Body (1:00–4:30) — *contemplative*

> *(slow)* "Every whole number has a digital root. Every digital root belongs to one of the three Family Number Groups.
>
> *[pause]*
>
> So every whole number belongs to one of three families.
>
> *[long pause]*
>
> Your year of birth has a digital root. Whichever digit it is, the year is in family one ({1, 4, 7}), family two ({2, 5, 8}), or family three ({3, 6, 9}).
>
> Your phone number's digital root has a family.
>
> Your address has a family.
>
> Your morning's number — temperature, time, anything — has a family.
>
> *[long pause — 12 seconds]*
>
> Numbers in family one (residue 1 mod 3): they cycle 1 → 2 → 4 in the Doubling Circuit before re-entering. They're 'forward-direction' family.
>
> Numbers in family two (residue 2 mod 3): they cycle 2 → 4 → 8 → 7 → 5 before re-entering. 'Compensating-direction' family.
>
> Numbers in family three (residue 0 mod 3): they don't enter the Doubling Circuit at all. They oscillate 3 ↔ 6 (if not 9) or stay fixed (if 9). The 'spirit-axis' family.
>
> *[pause]*
>
> So your numbers, by family, fall into three different *behavioural classes* under doubling. Family-one numbers spiral one way. Family-two numbers spiral the other way. Family-three numbers don't spiral; they oscillate or stand still.
>
> *[long pause]*
>
> Marko's framing — the families as *fields* — suggests these behavioural classes aren't arbitrary. They're three different *kinds* of numbers, with three different relationships to motion.
>
> *[pause]*
>
> Sit with the family-membership of one of your numbers. *Your year of birth.* Reduce. Identify the family. Notice what kind of number you've been carrying without naming it.
>
> *[long pause]*
>
> The Symbol now has family colours and family triangles on it. Your year-of-birth is now a *coloured vertex* in your peripheral attention.
>
> *[pause]*
>
> What's striking, contemplatively, about partitioning the integers into three families isn't *that* it can be done — any partition of an infinite set into three classes can be done. What's striking is that *this specific partition* — by residue mod 3, after digital-root reduction — corresponds to *behavioural classes* under the doubling map. Numbers in the same family *act the same way*. The partition is meaningful, not arbitrary.
>
> *[long pause]*
>
> Three behavioural classes for the integers. Every number you'll ever encounter is in one of three classes. Three families."
