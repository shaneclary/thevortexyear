> *Five components to sketch.*

> *Component 1 — coil specification.* *Strand count, dimensions, wire spec, phasing, counter-wind status*; *as detailed as source provides*; *flag what's missing*. *Engineering specification of the device under test*.
>
> *Component 2 — power input.* *Voltage source, frequency, waveform, current; how power-input is measured*; *whether RMS or peak; whether oscilloscope or multimeter*. *Input characterisation*.

> *Component 3 — load.* *Resistive load? Lamp? Capacitor? Tank circuit? Free-radiating?* *What absorbed the output*. *Output coupling*.

> *Component 4 — measurement instrumentation.* *What instruments were used to measure input and output power*; *calibration status if reported*; *measurement bandwidth and accuracy*. *Engineering quality of the measurement*.

> *Component 5 — environmental conditions.* *Ground; nearby conductors; EMI environment; specifically-controlled vs. uncontrolled*. *Environmental confounders identified*.
