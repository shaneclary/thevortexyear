> "Wk 33's single-strand winding asked one thing of the hand: *follow the helix*. *One path; one focus; one wire*. *Q3's first build was about presence with one task*.
>
> *[pause]*
>
> *Multi-strand winding asks more.*
>
> *Three things at once.* *Strand identity* — *which colour wire am I winding now?* *Phase offset* — *am I at the right entry point for this strand?* *Counter-wind direction* — *is this strand clockwise or anti-clockwise?*
>
> *Three simultaneous tracks; the hand manages all three*.
>
> *[pause]*
>
> *Three textures of multi-strand winding.*
>
> *Texture one — strand-switch moments.* *When strand 1 is fully wound and strand 2 begins, the hand sets down one wire and picks up another*. *Mental context-switch from 'strand 1's path' to 'strand 2's offset entry point and same path'*. *Brief pause; deliberate transition*.
>
> *Texture two — phase-marker checks.* *Before each strand begins, eye returns to the entry-point markings on the former*. *Verify position; verify offset; verify alignment with the canonical phase angle*. *More verification beats than single-strand.*
>
> *Texture three — counter-wind direction-tracking (if applied).* *If half strands wind one way, half the other, the hand's habituation must reset between groups*. *Direction is not a single decision but a per-strand decision*.
>
> *[pause]*
>
> *Two contrasts with Wk 33.*
>
> *Contrast one — focus shifts from one path to one path among many.* *Single-strand: full attention on the helix*. *Multi-strand: full attention on the current strand's helix, peripheral tracking of the others*. *Different cognitive load.*
>
> *Contrast two — duration and rhythm.* *Single-strand build session: ~60-90 minutes*. *Three-strand: ~3 hours*. *Six-strand: ~5-6 hours*. *Rhythm changes with duration; rest breaks become essential; sustained focus across multiple sessions becomes typical*.
>
> *Multi-strand build is meditative across hours, not minutes*.

> *[pause]*
>

> *Observation three — confidence accumulates.* *By multi-strand stage, single-strand is in muscle memory*; *practitioners trust their winding hand*; *complexity is navigable*. *Q3's engineering trajectory is being lived.*
