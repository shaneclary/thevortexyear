## 4. Body (1:00–4:30)

> "Mod A Lens 3 framed the session structure. *Today: walk through the session as a builder*.
>
> *[pause]*
>
> *Pre-session checklist (10 min).*
>
> *Decorated former: dimensions verified, surface clean, marking visible*.
>
> *Wire spools: cut to length per Mod A Lens 2 counts (~42 cm × strand count); each spool labelled with strand index and colour*.
>
> *Tools: wire stripper, magnet-wire varnish removal kit (sandpaper or chemical), soldering iron, heat-shrink tubing, flush cutter, magnifying lamp*.
>
> *Build log: page open, columns pre-written (Date, Strand Index, Phase Offset, Direction, Observations)*.
>
> *Workspace: bench cleared, lighting good, distraction-free for sustained focus session*.
>
> *Pre-session ready*.
>
> *[pause]*
>
> *Phase 1 — entry-point marking and angular verification (15-20 min).*
>
> *Mark entry points on outer equator of former at canonical phase angles*. *3-strand: 0°, 120°, 240°*. *6-strand: 0°, 60°, 120°, 180°, 240°, 300°*.
>
> *Verify with protractor; double-check each angle; note actual angles in log if any differ from canonical*. *Angular accuracy ±2° acceptable; ±5° flagged as anomaly*.
>
> *Annotate each entry-point with strand index*.
>
> *[pause]*
>
> *Phase 2 — strand 1 winding (~30-45 min).*
>
> *Strip ~3 cm of magnet wire varnish at lead end; thread through entry point; secure with masking tape at strand 1's first position*.
>
> *Follow strand 1's (col, row) sequence position-by-position around former (Mod B Lens 3 explicit construction)*.
>
> *Verify each position: 'col 1, row 0 — outer equator marked'; 'col 2, row 1 — verified visually'*.
>
> *Tighten wire as needed; correct kinks; flag and log anomalies*.
>
> *On closure: verify exit at strand 1's expected position (e.g., (0, 0) for canonical strand 1)*; *strip exit lead; tape down for measurement-stage attention*.
>
> *Log strand 1 entry: 'Strand 1: wound CW, [start time]–[end time], anomalies: [...]'*.
>
> *[pause]*
>
> *Phase 3 — strand 2, 3, ... N winding (per strand, ~30-45 min each).*
>
> *Repeat phase 2 for each remaining strand*.
>
> *For each strand, verify entry-point alignment with strand index before beginning*.
>
> *Strand-by-strand log entries*.
>
> *Inter-strand breaks (5-10 min between strands) help maintain focus*. *Multi-session if total session exceeds 3 hours*.
>
> *[pause]*
>
> *Phase 4 — counter-wind verification (if applied) (5-10 min).*
>
> *If specification includes counter-winding, verify direction per strand by inspection*; *strand directions match canonical specification*.
>
> *Log direction column for each strand*.
>
> *[pause]*
>
> *Phase 5 — closure verification, continuity checks, and log finalisation (15-20 min).*
>
> *Per-strand continuity check: multimeter on each strand's two leads; expected DC R within 5% of single-strand baseline (~0.4 Ω for ~42 cm of 1.0 mm copper wire)*. **`[ORG-INPUT]`** for canonical baseline R.
>
> *Per-pair short-check: leads of strand i should not short to leads of strand j*; *isolation expected*; *if measured, document and investigate*.
>
> *Visual inspection: all strands appear correctly wound; entry-points visually equispaced*.
>
> *Log finalisation: build complete, anomalies summarised, total session time noted*.
>
> *Build session done; coil ready for measurement phase*.
>
> *[pause]*
>
> *Three engineering judgments during the session.*
>
> *Judgment one — when to retraversse vs. when to log anomaly.* *Minor kinks (visual but not structural) get logged and continued past*; *major kinks (impact wire path significantly) get retraversed and logged with both attempts*. *Engineering judgment.*
>
> *Judgment two — when to break.* *6-strand session is long; force-completion fatigues attention; quality drops*. *Plan multi-session if session exceeds 3 hours*; *break between strands or between strand-pairs*. *Engineering pacing.*
>
> *Judgment three — when an anomaly is build-failure.* *Most anomalies are build-flavour and proceed*; *some (significantly off-spec entry point, broken wire mid-strand) require restart of that strand*. *Threshold: ~5° entry-point error or wire damage*. *Engineering threshold*.
>
> *[pause]*
>
> Practical takeaway. *Multi-strand build session: pre-session checklist, entry-point marking, strand-by-strand winding (~30-45 min per strand), counter-wind verification, closure check.* *Three engineering judgments: anomaly-handling, pacing, restart threshold.* *Build log throughout; quality preserved.* **`[ORG-INPUT]`** for canonical baseline R."
