## 4. Body (1:00–4:30)

> "Topology is fixed. *The (1, n) torus knot is the Rodin Coil's topological signature; we can't change it without ceasing to build a Rodin Coil*.
>
> *Strand count is mostly fixed by tradition*. *3- or 6-strand for course-canonical builds; convention-driven; not free*.
>
> *Phasing is different.*
>
> *[pause]*
>
> *Phasing is the engineer's variable.*
>
> *Equispaced phasing is the default*. *0°, 60°, 120°, 180°, 240°, 300° for 6-strand*. *0°, 120°, 240° for 3-strand*.
>
> *But the engineer can choose otherwise*. *Non-equispaced phasing*: *e.g., 0°, 30°, 60°, 180°, 210°, 240°*. *Cluster of 3 strands close, gap, cluster of 3 strands close*. *Same strand count; different phasing; different coil.*
>
> *[pause]*
>
> *What changes when phasing changes.*
>
> *Field structure changes.* *Equispaced: 6-fold rotational symmetry*. *Non-equispaced clustered: 2-fold or lower symmetry*. *Field pattern reflects phasing pattern*.
>
> *Mutual inductance pattern changes.* *Equispaced: identical mutual inductances between adjacent strand pairs*. *Non-equispaced: mutual inductances vary by strand-pair separation*. *Frequency response shifts*.
>
> *Engineering use changes.* *Equispaced: maximally symmetric field; antenna with omnidirectional pattern*. *Clustered: field-shaped; antenna with directional pattern*. *Different applications.*
>
> *[pause]*
>
> *Three contemplations on phasing as variable.*
>
> *Contemplation one — the engineer's freedom is real.* *The decorated former (Wk 26) doesn't dictate phasing*. *The doubling cycle suggests equispaced phasing because the doubling-cycle positions are equispaced on the lattice — but this is a default, not a constraint*. *Engineering judgment shapes phasing.*
>
> *Contemplation two — convention has reasons.* *Equispaced phasing produces maximum symmetry*; *VBM-correspondence is preserved at the named-position level*; *measurement is most predictable*. *Convention is well-motivated*. *Departures from convention are deliberate engineering choices*.
>
> *Contemplation three — phasing is where VBM-engineering touches engineering-decision-making.* *Single-strand had little decision space*. *Multi-strand introduces phasing*. *Engineering judgment sharpens here*. *The framework becomes engineerable in the engineer's hands*.
>
> *[pause]*

>
> *Most VBM content has felt fixed*. *The Symbol of Enlightenment is what it is; the doubling cycle is what it is; the helix on the torus is what it is*.

> *[pause]*

>
> *Reflection one — phasing is the first place the engineer becomes a designer.* *Up to multi-strand, the engineer has been building to specification*. *With phasing, the engineer can specify*. *Authorship begins.*

> *Reflection two — the framework supports designer authorship.* *VBM provides default phasings (equispaced, doubling-cycle-correspondent)*; *the engineer can adopt defaults or depart from them*. *The framework is generative, not restrictive*. *Q3 enables engineering creativity.*
