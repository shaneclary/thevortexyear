> *Three countable features.*
>
> *Feature 1 — turns.* *Number of times the wire wraps around the torus's major circle*. *For one cycle = 1 major turn (Wks 27 Mod A Lens 2)*. *Multiple cycles = multiple turns*.
>
> *Specifications*: *single-cycle coil → 1 major turn. Three-cycle coil → 3 major turns. Nine-cycle coil → 9 major turns.* *Turns determine wire length and inductance.*
>
> *Feature 2 — strands.* *Number of independent wires wound on the same former*. *Single-strand → 1 wire. Multi-strand → 2, 4, 6, or 8 wires (Wk 28 Mod C Lens 2 build levels)*. *Strands determine multi-coupled coil designs.*
>
> *Feature 3 — terminations.* *Number of wire-ends emerging from the coil for connection*. *Each strand has 2 ends → 2 terminations per strand*. *Single-strand: 2 terminations. Two-strand: 4. Six-strand: 12.* *Connection points; terminal block design.*
>
> *Three features; quantitative measure of coil's complexity*.
>
> *[pause]*
>
> *Worked example — typical first-build coil.*
>
> *Specifications*: *single-strand; one cycle; basic build*.

> *Strands: 1 strand.*

> *[pause]*
