> Powell, on frequencies: *'Don't operate the coil at random frequencies. Identify self-resonant frequency first; pick operating frequency based on what you want to do. > *Powell on replication ethics.*

> Powell, on replication: *'When someone else builds a Rodin Coil and tests it as antenna, they should get similar results to yours within engineering tolerance. If they don't, either your build or theirs has documentation gaps. Replication ethics: document everything in your build log; share methodology; expect others to find slightly different numbers; embrace replication as the engineering reality. The 60% claim or any claim is meaningful only if multiple practitioners independently confirm it within tolerance. Solo measurement is engineering data point one; community measurement is engineering knowledge.'*
