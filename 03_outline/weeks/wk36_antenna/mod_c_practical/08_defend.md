> *The honest engineering defence.*
>
> *Documented honest measurements — confirming, disconfirming, ambiguous — are the gold of replication. Engineering and science advance when claims survive multiple independent measurements. A single measurement is data point one; multiple measurements aggregate into engineering knowledge. Practitioners who measure honestly and document thoroughly contribute the gold; practitioners who claim without measuring or measure without documenting do not contribute. Wk 36's data is gold of replication regardless of whether it confirms or disconfirms.*

> *[pause]*

> *Three reasons documented honest measurement is gold.*
>
> *Reason 1 — replication is the scientific/engineering currency.* *Claims that haven't been independently replicated are unverified*; *replication is what converts claim to knowledge*. *A claim that 'works for the originator' is anecdote*; *a claim independently replicated is engineering*.
>
> *Reason 2 — disconfirming results are equally informative.* *In all of science and engineering, disconfirming honest measurements are as valuable as confirming ones*; *they bound claim scope, identify configuration-dependence, and prevent over-claiming*. *Disconfirmations save practitioners and resources from chasing unfounded specifications*. *Engineering field benefits*.
>
> *Reason 3 — ambiguous results identify methodology gaps.* *Measurements that don't clearly confirm or disconfirm point at methodology improvements*; *better instrumentation, better calibration, better setup*; *engineering methodology refines through ambiguous-data feedback*.
>
> *Three reasons; replication value general*.
>
> *[pause]*
>
> *Three things distinguish gold-of-replication from non-gold contribution.*
>
> *Distinguish 1 — documentation completeness.* *Gold has full setup specification, methodology, raw data, error analysis, limitations*; *non-gold has only headline result*. *Wk 36's deliverable is gold by documentation criteria*.
>
> *Distinguish 2 — methodology defensibility.* *Gold has methodology that can withstand peer review*; *four-pillar methodology (Mod B Lens 8) is defensible*. *Wk 36's deliverable is gold by methodology criteria*.
>
> *Distinguish 3 — interpretation calibration.* *Gold's conclusions match measurements*; *no over-claim either direction*. *Wk 36's honest-reporting commitment maintains calibration*.
>
> *Three criteria; gold-of-replication identified*.

> *Defend 1.* *Documented honest measurement is the engineering field's gold of replication regardless of outcome*. *Defendable*.
>
> *Defend 2.* *Disconfirming and ambiguous results are as valuable as confirming results*. *Defendable*.
>
> *Defend 3.* *Replication is engineering knowledge's currency; multiple measurements triangulate truth*. *Defendable*.
