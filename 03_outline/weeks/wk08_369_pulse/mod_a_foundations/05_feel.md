## 0. Authoring notes
*A trinary, not binary, heartbeat.* Contemplative beat on the difference between binary (on/off) and trinary (3-6-9 with the 9 as still centre).

## 1. Outcome
Student has spent quiet time with the trinary structure of the Pulse.

## 2. Hook (0:00–0:30)
*[A heartbeat trace, then the 3-6-9 pulse — three states rather than two.]*

> *(slow)* "A binary heartbeat is on, off. A trinary heartbeat is on, off, *still*. Sit with that."

## 3. Setup (0:30–1:00)
> "Lens 5 of 9. Six minutes."

## 4. Body (1:00–4:30) — *contemplative*

> *(slow)* "A heartbeat is binary. *Beat. Rest. Beat. Rest.* Two states alternating.
>
> *[pause]*
>
> The 3-6-9 Pulse isn't binary. It has three states. *3. 6. 9.*
>
> *[long pause]*
>
> The 3 and 6 alternate, like a binary pulse — *3, 6, 3, 6, 3, 6.* The 9 is the still centre — present, fixed, not alternating.
>
> *[pause]*
>
> Together: a binary oscillation around a fixed centre. *On the binary axis*: 3 and 6 alternating. *Off the binary axis*: 9 holding still.
>
> Three states. Two of them moving; one of them still.
>
> *[long pause — 12 seconds]*
>
> Most physical heartbeats are binary. Pendulum swings. Sine waves. AC current. The world is full of two-state oscillators.
>
> Trinary structures are rarer in our experience. *Yes / no / maybe.* *Past / future / present.* *Doer / done-to / observer.* Three-position systems with a still witness.
>
> *[pause]*
>
> Marko reads the 9 as that still witness. Not part of the alternation, but containing it. The doubling cycle (which doesn't visit 9) and the polar pairs (which don't include 9) and the family alternation (between 3 and 6) all *happen around* the 9. Stillness as the observer of motion.
>
> *[long pause]*
>
> Whether you read this metaphysically — *Spirit as witness*, in Marko's framing — or just structurally — *9 as fixed point of the doubling map, the only element invariant under multiplication by 2* — the asymmetry is there. The 9 is *different from* 3 and 6 even though all three are in the same family. The math distinguishes it.
>
> *[pause]*
>
> Sit with the trinary structure for a moment. Two moving, one still. The Symbol has this asymmetry built in, mathematically forced.
>
> *[long pause]*
>
> What you're holding contemplatively — *binary alternation around a still centre* — is a structure rare enough that most cultures have specific names for it. Yin and Yang with the Tao at the centre. The dancer and the danced with the awareness that watches. The wave and the trough with the stillness between.
>
> *[pause]*
>
> Three states. Two oscillating. One still. The 3-6-9 Pulse."
