> *Pattern 2 — toroidal coil (mainstream).* *Toroidal former; wire wound around the tube*, perpendicular to the major-circle direction. The mainstream toroidal pattern. *Used in toroidal transformers, RF chokes, some specialised inductors.*

> *Properties:* Magnetic field largely contained inside the torus; low external-field interference; specific topology.
