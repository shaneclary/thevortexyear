> *"An inverse is a kind of symmetry. When two operations cancel each other, you have found a place where the structure is invariant under exchange. Halving and doubling cancel — apply both, you are back where you started. That cancellation is not a triviality. It is a sign that the underlying object — the cycle on the Symbol — has a built-in mirror. Most operations on most systems do not have inverses; you cannot un-do most things. The math underneath the Symbol does. That reversibility is what physics calls *time symmetry* — the kind of symmetry that energy-conserving systems have. The Doubling Circuit is reversible because halving exists. The reversibility is part of why I read the cycle as a candidate for an energy-conserving structure underlying physical systems. The cancellation of doubling and halving isn't a workaround; it is evidence."*
