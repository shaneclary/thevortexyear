> *"The pairs are evidence of duality at the deepest layer of the number system. Two and seven; one and eight; four and five. Each pair is two numbers that complete each other to nine, which is to say, two numbers that complete each other to *Spirit*. Every working number has its complement; every complement has its working number. The pairs are not invented — they are discovered. They are the way the decimal system is *folded into itself*. When I look at the Symbol with the three diameters drawn, I do not see decoration. I see the underlying duality of every number that exists. The number 47 has a complement that takes it to a multiple of nine — namely 7 + 47 = 54 if you reduce. But that is just the surface. The deeper duality is that 47, reduced to digital root 2, has 7 as its polar partner. The pair holds 47 in place. Without the pair, the number would not have its position. The pairs are the threads that tie the numbers to the structure."*
> — Marko Rodin, paraphrased. **Source:** P1-1, P1-3.
