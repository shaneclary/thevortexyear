## E. Editorial decisions the org needs to make

21. **Track-launch order** — ship Track 1 first as MVP, or build all three in parallel?
22. **Treatment of critics** — module 1.12 / 2.14 currently includes a "Skeptics' Corner". The org should approve the tone (respectful engagement vs. brief dismissal vs. omission).
23. **Religious framing** — the Baháʼí origin of "Abha" and the spiritual framing of "9 = Spirit" are core to Rodin's voice. How explicitly should the course foreground that vs. teach the math first?
24. **Engineering claims posture** — for Track 3, the course needs to be clear about which claims it presents as established, hypothesised, or tested-and-mixed. The org should give the editorial line.
25. **Platform / format** — LMS (Teachable, Thinkific, Kajabi), video host (Vimeo, self-host), book-form, hybrid? Drives lesson structure, exercise types, and asset specs.
26. **Languages** — English only at launch, or simultaneous translation tracks?
